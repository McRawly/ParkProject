Conium Maculatum


Poison Hemlock

Eating even a small amount of any part of this plant is deadly, it is also toxic to the skin and respiratory system.

Symptoms: pupil dilation, dizziness, and trembling followed by slowing of the heartbeat, paralysis of the central nervous system, muscle paralysis, and death due to respiratory failure.
